Located in the heart of Ottawa’s Little Italy, DreamLand Cafe offers incredible fresh pasta dishes, made with love and finesse.

 

DreamLand Cafe opened its first location in May 2017 located in the downtown core on Laurier Ave. Disguised by the grey buildings surrounding it, DreamLand is filled with bright colours, warmth and overwhelmingly delicious smells. Since the beginning, owners (and sisters) Marlo and Coco De Leo, have created an inviting atmosphere for clientele. The food however, is what brings customers back.

The Ottawa native sisters elevate classic Italian family recipes. Some outstanding signature dishes include their Spaghetti & Meatballs, Carbonara, and Pesto Mac n’ Cheese. Their newer and bigger restaurant that opened May 2018 on Preston St., offers full service dining, craft cocktails, vintage wine, patio seating, and more.
